New Lil Yachty Song "Minnesota"

In “Minnesota,” Yatchy’s deceptively simple new song since last year’s equally fresh “Lil Boat,” his cheerful hook (“You need to stay out of the streets if you can’t take the heat/ Cause it get cold like Minnesota”) props up in between free-form verses. The song is part ode to the thirty-second U.S. state, part nonchalant flex on the haters. There are piano notes that hang like some chillingly sharp breeze, low-frequency bass rumbles practically ready to avalanche, and ice-cold lines from the rapper that convey the trademark toughness of contemporary Atlanta street rap.
